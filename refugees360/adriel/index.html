Inside the Lives of Asylum Seekers in Germany

Escaping Persecution

ADRIEL, 30 years-old, is from Iran. She has been in Germany since November 2015 and as of March, 2016 she has been living in the UK.

Click to see 360 video

Adriel’s journey to Germany begins long before 2015. In 2007 she left Iran with her then-husband to pursue higher studies in Malaysia. After giving birth to her son and divorcing her husband she returned to Iran.

Suffering the stigma of being a divorced mother, she realized that she wanted a better life for both her and her son. She returned to Malaysia to finish her studies. In Malaysia her son was diagnosed as being autistic and hyperactive (ADHD). Also while in Malaysia she found a new religion and converted to Christianity. Upon returning to Iran she feared her husband would try to take her son away so she used fake Brazilian papers to travel from Iran to Budapest. The authorities caught her, but didn’t send her back and she and her son arrived in Germany in November 2015.

She now lives in the United Kingdom.

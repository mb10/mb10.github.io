Melissa Bosworth
I'm an energy and environmental journalist. I've covered renewable energy throughout the US and Latin America, and I co-founded Solar Power Report, a solar news start-up that recently won a Sunshot Catalyst grant from the US DOE. I’m focusing on multimedia and data journalism at UC Berkeley’s Graduate School of Journalism.
